About us

Abc Advisory Company creates learning solutions that separate the signal from the noise.

We bring together experts in research, evaluation, technology, and design to find new approaches to age-old MEL and research problems. We’re creative and innovative, experimenting with technologies and techniques beyond those traditionally used in international development.

Our team is bound by the belief that knowledge should be shared and co-created. We value responsible data collection, centering local knowledge, and democratizing development practices.

Biography:

Juan Pablo Castro currently works as Senior Program Excellence Advisor for DT Global, a major implementer of international development programs. In his current role, JP is supporting the roll out of multiple institutional initiatives to improve management practices and procedures, learning, and innovation. Previously he served in senior leadership roles for different conflict, stabilization, and political transition programs in various parts of the Middle East, Africa, and Latin America. JP has a bachelors’ degree in political science and economics from the University of Florida, and masters’ degrees in public policy and business administration from Princeton University and IE Business School.
