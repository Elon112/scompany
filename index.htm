01.
Strategy and design
Frameworks and methodologies for learning, M&E, and knowledge management.
What we do:

Whether during start-up or close out, we support programs with developing learning strategies so that they’re better equipped to capture insights and improve programming results. Abc Advisory Company provides technical guidance in:

  • Organizational Learning
  • Collaboration, Learning and Adapting
  • Knowledge Management
  • Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL)
What this looks like:
  • Research and learning agendas
  • Monitoring and evaluation plans
  • Theories of Change (TOCs)
  • Data collection systems
  • Feedback and listening mechanisms
02.
Training, facilitation, and mentorship
Customized learning support designed to meet diverse needs.
What we do:

We create spaces for learning and reflection through technical training and facilitated learning events. We specialize in using media and technology to deliver tailored solutions in complex, remotely managed environments.

What this looks like:
  • MEL/research technical assistance and training
  • E-learning platforms
  • Training needs assessments
  • Reflection sessions
  • Strategic planning and implementation retreats
  • Partner mentorship to achieve MEL goals

03.
Tools and products
Purpose-built resources for program learning and communications.
What we do:

Leveraging our network of MEL practitioners, designers, GIS specialists, and technologists, we develop multimedia tools and products for data collection, organization, analysis, and presentation.

What this looks like:
  • Data collection tools and platforms
  • Performance management systems
  • E-learning platforms
  • Digital dashboards
  • Data visualizations such as infographics, maps, diagrams, and timelines
  • Videos to communicate programming and research results
